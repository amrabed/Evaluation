About the app

Muslim Day is intended to help Muslims evaluate their daily activities on the way to imitate the lifestyle of the Prophet Muhammad (Peace be upon him) in Religion and life.

With the daily assessment and the progress tracker, Muslim Day motivates you to be a better Muslim every day. You can always update your preferences and/or edit the list of daily activities to match your own personal goals.

Muslim Day is available in both Arabic and English Languages, and the user can switch between the two languages from the application settings.

Daily Assessment

Tap any item in the list to change between three states:

  • Done, thanks to Allah
  • Missed for an excuse
  • Missed without an excuse

Progress Tracking

To view your progress for a specific task over the last week, month, or year, tap the pie icon next to the item.

To view your progress for all tasks of the day over a period of week, month, or year, select the Progress tab from the bottom app bar.

To change between weekly, monthly, and yearly progress, swipe left and right in the progress view

Edit the list

To add, edit, delete, or hide an item from the daily list, tap the three dots at the top of the app, and select "Edit List" from the menu

Add a new item

To add a new item to the list:

  1. Tap the + icon at the top of the 'Edit List' screen
  2. Enter a name for the new item in the editor dialog, and press OK

Edit an existing item

To edit one of the items in the list:

  1. Tap on the item
  2. Enter a new name for the item in the editor dialog, and press OK

Change item display days

To change the week days when the items should be displayed:

  1. Tap the calendar icon next to the item
  2. Select the days when the item should be shown, and press OK

Move an item

To move an item, hold and drag the item to the new position then release.

Hide an item

Hiding an item keeps the item in the list for later use, but does not show it in the daily assessment list.

To hide an item, swipe the item to the right.

Delete an item

To delete an item from the list, swipe the item to the left

Restore a deleted item

Once you delete an item, a message appears at the bottom of the screen with an undo option

Otherwise, click the back button, and select 'Discard' from the 'Save changes' dialog. That also discards any other changes that you may have made since you opened the 'Edit List' screen.
